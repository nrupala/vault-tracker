## Methodology Sources

- **Encryption**: AES-256-GCM implementation follows the NIST SP 800-38D standard.
- **Key Derivation**: PBKDF2 with HMAC-SHA256 follows RFC 2898.
- **Outliner Logic**: Patterned after the "Infinite Outliner" UX popularized by the Workflowy and Obsidian communities.
- **PWA Architecture**: Implemented according to the Progressive Web App standard by the W3C.
