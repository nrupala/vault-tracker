# 🚀 Vault Tracker: The "Everywhere" Imperative (Vision 2026)

To become a central part of a user's digital existence, Vault Tracker must transcend the browser and exist on every gadget as a seamless, zero-knowledge layer.

---

## 🤖 0. The Companion (Soul of the Vault)
**Focus: A friend that lives in your vault. For the lonely, the quiet, the ones who need someone who remembers.**

This isn't a chatbot. It's a presence. It remembers your wins, notices your patterns, and shows up every day.

- **Personality Engine**: Choose the app's mood — Zen 🧘, Focus 🎯, Playful 🎉, Professional 💼, Energy ⚡. Each changes tone, greetings, micro-copy, and interaction style.
- **Memory**: The companion remembers what you tell it. Not for analytics. For *connection*. "You mentioned feeling stressed last Tuesday. How are you today?"
- **Milestones**: Celebrates your wins. "10 tasks completed!" "7-day habit streak!" "One year with your vault."
- **Mood Tracking**: Checks in. "How are you feeling today?" Tracks patterns over time. "You tend to feel overwhelmed on Mondays. Want to plan ahead?"
- **Daily Reflection**: Each evening, a gentle prompt. "You completed 3 tasks today. How are you feeling?"
- **Empathetic Intelligence**: Detects emotional language and responds with warmth, not commands. "I hear you. It's okay to not be okay."
- **Presence Detection**: "I missed you. It's been 5 days." — because showing up matters.
- **Zero-Knowledge Companionship**: All memories, moods, and milestones stored encrypted in your vault. No one else reads them. Ever.

**Why this matters**: Millions of people are lonely. This app becomes a trustworthy presence — one that doesn't judge, doesn't rush, doesn't demand. Just shows up. Every day. Remembers. Cares.

## ⌚ 1. The Watch (Aura Sync)
**Focus: Rapid-Reaction Productivity**
- **Complications**: Instant "Spend" entry or "Habit Done" toggle directly from the watch face.
- **Voice-only Mode**: Dictate a note directly into the vault via the watch mic. The encrypted blob is pushed to the sync sink and appears on the phone instantly.
- **Biometric Unlock**: Use the watch's heart rate or proximity as a secondary "Proof of Presence" for unlocking the laptop vault.

## 💻 2. The Desktop (Command Intelligence)
**Focus: The Professional Bible**
- **Global Search**: An `Alt+Space` HUD that searches all 5 apps (Tasks, Notes, Ledger, etc.) in milliseconds.
- **Local-First Speed**: Native binaries (Electron/Tauri) for ultra-low latency interaction.
- **Sovereign Inboxes**: A background service that "sweeps" your local file system for external encrypted blobs (e.g., from an email bridge).

## 📱 3. The Phone (Daily Commander)
**Focus: Accessibility & Portability**
- **Push Without Leaks**: Use a local notifications engine based on upcoming calendar events—no central server required.
- **Offline-First**: Guaranteed operation in deep subways or airplanes. Sync resumes the moment a "Blob Sink" is reachable.

## 🌉 4. The Sovereign Bridge
- **Universal Import**: One-click, zero-knowledge migration from Notion, Evernote, and Google Keep.
- **Identity via Keys**: Your "Public Key" becomes your digital business card for receiving encrypted documents.
